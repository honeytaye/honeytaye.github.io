Project 01 · Automotive Analytics
Forecasting Clustering Classification Multi-Region

BMW Global Sales Analysis & Predictive Modeling (2010–2024)

Full-cycle analysis on 14 years of BMW vehicle sales data across six global regions. Three business questions addressed through three distinct modeling approaches, delivering strategic intelligence for market planning, product development, and customer targeting.

Sales Forecasting — Applied ARIMA time-series modeling to historical yearly totals, projecting 2025 global sales at 16.54 million units. Wide prediction intervals surfaced market volatility, informing flexible production planning.
Customer Segmentation — Multi-feature K-Means clustering (K=3) using fuel type, transmission, region, and model — identifying three behavioral segments: traditional petrol, diesel-hybrid transition, and full EV adopters.
Sales Classification — Logistic regression classifier tested whether vehicle specs predict sales volume. 50.4% accuracy confirmed specs are not a driver, redirecting strategic focus to macroeconomic and regional factors.

Project 03 · Real Estate Analytics
Tableau SQL Excel Geospatial

Calgary Real Estate Market Analysis & Pricing Intelligence

Conducted a comprehensive analysis of Calgary residential property listings and sales records to identify pricing trends, neighbourhood-level demand patterns, and factors influencing days-on-market — providing actionable intelligence for property investment and valuation decisions.

SQL Profiling — Queried and cleaned a 50,000+ record property dataset using SQL Server, applying data validation rules to standardize address fields, remove duplicate listings, and calculate derived metrics such as price-per-square-foot by neighbourhood and property type.
Excel Regression Model — Built a multiple regression model in Excel to identify the strongest predictors of sale price — finding that square footage, proximity to LRT stations, and garage availability collectively explained 71% of price variance across the dataset.
Tableau Geospatial Dashboard — Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard mapping median sale prices, listing density, and average days-on-market by neighbourhood. Heat map visualizations highlighted emerging high-demand zones in the NW and SE quadrants.
