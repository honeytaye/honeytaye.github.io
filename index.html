03 — Projects

Project 01 · Automotive Analytics
Forecasting Clustering Classification Multi-Region

BMW Global Sales Analysis & Predictive Modeling (2010–2024)

Full-cycle analysis on 14 years of BMW vehicle sales data across six global regions — Africa, Asia, Europe, the Middle East, North America, and South America. Three business questions addressed through three distinct modeling approaches, delivering strategic intelligence for market planning, product development, and customer targeting.

Sales Forecasting — ARIMA
Applied ARIMA time-series modeling to historical yearly totals (2010–2024), projecting 2025 global sales at 16.54 million units. The wide prediction interval surfaced significant market volatility, informing the need for flexible, scenario-based production planning rather than a single-point forecast.
Customer Segmentation — K-Means (K=3)
Multi-feature K-Means clustering using fuel type, transmission, region, and model — revealing three behavioral segments: Traditional Petrol drivers, Diesel/Hybrid transition customers, and Full EV adopters. Price-only clustering proved ineffective given BMW's narrow premium price band, validating the need for technology-preference segmentation.
Sales Classification — Logistic Regression
Logistic regression classifier tested whether vehicle specs (engine size, color, model) predict high vs. low sales volume. A 50.4% accuracy — identical to random chance — confirmed specs have zero statistical impact on sales, redirecting strategic focus toward macroeconomic and regional market factors.
